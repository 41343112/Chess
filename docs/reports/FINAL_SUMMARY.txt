HIGHLIGHTING SYSTEM:
  Priority 1: RED (#FF6B6B) - King in check
  Priority 2: GOLD (#FFD700) - Selected piece
  Priority 3: GREEN (#90EE90) - Valid moves
  Priority 4: CREAM/BROWN - Default board

INTERACTION METHODS:
  Method 1: Click-to-Move (existing)
    1. Click piece → highlights valid moves
    2. Click destination → move executes
  
  Method 2: Drag-and-Drop (NEW)
    1. Click and hold piece → highlights valid moves
    2. Drag to destination → shows pixmap
    3. Drop → move executes

  Both methods work simultaneously!
